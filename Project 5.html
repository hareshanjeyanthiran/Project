Veg Biryani

  1. For the Rice

    • 1.5 cups Basmati rice
    • 4 cups water
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 2-3 green cardamoms
    • 1 bay leaf
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • ½ tsp salt

  2. For the Vegetable Gravy

    • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
    • 1 large onion (thinly sliced)
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 large tomato (chopped)
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potatoes, etc.)
    • ½ cup yogurt (curd)
    • Garam Masala
