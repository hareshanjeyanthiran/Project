Welcome to A’soud Global School (AGS) Duqm

A’soud Global School (AGS) Duqm is Duqm’s first international school opened in September 2019 intended to offer Cambridge International Curriculum

About Our Curriculum

AGS Duqm aims to provide a high quality British style primary education and adapted to meet the varied needs of our international school community. The curriculum is designed to encourage individuals to reach their full potential and inspires them to create their own future.
